opt:
  --date-format=FORMAT
 
FORMAT is a combination of:
  yyyy   4-digit year
  MM     2-digit month
  MMM    3-char month name (e.g. Jan, Feb)
  dd     2-digit day (1 - 31)
  ddd    3-char week (e.g. Mon, Tue)
  HH     2-digit hour (in 24-hours format)
  mm     2-digit minute
  ss     2-digit second

For example,
   --date-format=yyyy-MM-dd
  "--date-format=MMM dd, yyyy HH:mm"
