opt: --sort=name|size|date|last|count
 
1. --sort=name  list items by sorting of the name.
2. --sort=size  list items by sorting of the size.
3. --sort=date  list items by sorting of the date/time.
4. --sort=last  list grouping items by sorting of the last date/time.
5. --sort=count list grouping items by sorting of the count.
